Commission Process & Payment

-> Contact me via social media or e-mail, whichever you prefer.

-> Please include a description of what you would like along with character reference images.

-> I won't reply to messages without details or references.

-> Full payment before starting.

-> I'll send updates at each stage of the process for feedback and revisions.

-> Average turnaround time: 1 - 3 working days.

Please Note

-> My main strengths are fantasy and modern character illustration. Other genres are fine but I may not have as much experience in them.

-> I won't draw erotic or pornographic commissions but nudity may be okay depending on the context.

-> If I don't accept your commission, please don't contact me to ask why and please don't resend the same commission request.


Thank you!
